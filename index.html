About

I am Yuliang Li, a Ph.D. student in Intelligent Science and Technology at Beijing University of Posts and Telecommunications (BUPT), advised by Prof. Heng Guo.

I received my B.Eng. degree in Intelligent Science and Technology from BUPT. My research focuses on vision-language models and scene understanding.

As a project lead or core member, I have received 5 national-level awards and 8 provincial/ministerial-level awards.
